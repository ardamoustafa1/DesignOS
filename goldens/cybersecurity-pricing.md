# Golden: Cybersecurity Pricing

Use this as the target bar for EDR/SIEM pricing pages.

## Brief

Design a pricing page for a cybersecurity SaaS selling to CISOs and security engineers.

## Load

- `references/cybersecurity-dark-ui.md`
- `patterns/pricing.md`
- `industries/cybersecurity.md`
- `PROOF_STANDARD.md`

## Must Show

- honest fictional-brand disclosure unless customers are real
- seat/unit economics
- trial and procurement anxiety reducers
- comparison table
- architecture proof instead of fake testimonials

## Failure To Catch

Fake "Trusted by" logo strips and certification claims without source.
