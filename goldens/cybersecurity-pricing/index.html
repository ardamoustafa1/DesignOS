Procurement questions, answered before the call

What exactly counts as a "seat"?

A named analyst who can act on alerts — triage, close, escalate, edit rules. Read-only dashboard viewers (management, auditors) are free and unlimited on every plan.

What happens when the trial ends?

The workspace locks read-only for 30 days — detections stop, but your rules, cases, and evidence stay exportable. Nothing is deleted silently.

Can we run a proof-of-value on real endpoints?

Yes — the 14-day trial is the full Pro product on up to 200 real endpoints. Extension for a structured PoV with acceptance criteria is a one-email ask.

How does annual billing and true-up work?

Annual is billed up front for committed seats/endpoints; growth mid-term is trued up quarterly at your contracted per-unit rate — never at list price as a gotcha.

Security review, DPA, and questionnaires?

Enterprise includes a named engineer for your review process. Standard DPA and architecture docs ship on request at any tier — before you pay, not after.

How do we leave?

Export rules, cases, and evidence bundles in open formats at any time. Offboarding is documented in the docs like any other feature — lock-in is not our retention strategy.
