# Investor Demo Page Recipe

```text
Design an investor-facing demo page for an early-stage AI product.

Audience: seed-stage investors and technical angels.
Goal: communicate the product's wedge, proof, and market insight in 90 seconds.
Tone: sharp, credible, evidence-first.

Load:
- DesignOS/industries/ai-startup.md
- DesignOS/patterns/landing-pages.md
- DesignOS/patterns/comparison-pages.md
- DesignOS/psychology/trust.md
- DesignOS/scoring/rubric.md

Requirements:
- Lead with real product transformation: input -> output.
- Separate measured traction from hypothesis.
- Use screenshots or workflow proof instead of abstract AI visuals.
- No invented logos, revenue, waitlist counts, or benchmark claims.
- End with a six-dimension scorecard and redo anything under 95.
```
