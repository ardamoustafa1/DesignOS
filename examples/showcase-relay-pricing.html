Questions, answered honestly

What happens when my trial ends?

You drop to Free automatically — nothing is deleted, nothing is charged. Rotations beyond the Free limits pause until you upgrade or trim them.

How do seat changes get billed?

Adding a seat is prorated to the day; removing one credits your next invoice. No calls required — it's a slider in Settings → Billing.

What happens if I downgrade?

You keep read access to everything you created. Features above your new plan pause rather than vanish, and your full history exports any time.

Do you charge per incident or per alert?

Never. Usage-based pricing on incidents would mean profiting from your bad days. Seats only.

Can we pay by invoice / PO?

Yes — annual Pro (10+ seats) and Enterprise can pay by invoice. VAT handled per region at checkout.
