Questions

Before you install it

Do we have to rip out our existing OTel setup?

No. If you're already emitting OpenTelemetry spans, point your existing exporter at Tracewise's collector endpoint. No new SDK, no re-instrumentation.

What happens to our data if we self-host and something breaks?

Traces are stored in open Parquet files on disk you control — readable with standard tooling even if Tracewise itself is down. Nothing is locked in a proprietary format.

How does tail-based sampling avoid losing the trace I actually need?

Every trace is buffered briefly before the sampling decision. Errors, timeouts, and latency outliers are always retained at 100%; only clean, fast, boring traces get sampled down.

Is there a free tier for the managed service?

Yes — free up to 10GB of ingested spans/month, no card required. Self-hosting the open-core build has no volume limit at all.

Where do I see uptime history before trusting this in production?

The status page above links to full incident history, not just a green dot — check it before you rely on us for the traces that explain your incidents.
