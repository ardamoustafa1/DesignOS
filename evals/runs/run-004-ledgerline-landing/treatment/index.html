Questions

Before you start the trial

What does it cost after the trial?

Pricing is per connected client account, billed monthly or annually. You'll see the exact number for your agency size before entering payment details — nothing is hidden behind a "contact sales" wall.

Is our financial data secure?

Bank connections use read-only, encrypted access — Ledgerline never has the ability to move money. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Can we import our existing spreadsheets?

Yes. CSV import maps your existing categories and clients during onboarding, so historical records carry over instead of starting from zero.

How does this work across multiple clients?

Each client gets its own ledger and reconciliation feed under one agency account, so nothing crosses between client books by accident.

What happens when the trial ends?

You'll get a reminder three days before billing starts. If you don't continue, your data stays exportable for 30 days before removal.
