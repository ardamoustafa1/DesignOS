# Reference Pack: Stripe-Level Marketing

Use for B2B landing pages, pricing pages, launch pages, and enterprise product sites.

## Taste Signature

- Dense but calm information hierarchy.
- Product proof above decorative branding.
- Strong section rhythm: hero, proof, workflow, depth, objection handling.
- Large type with crisp body copy, never vague hype.
- Gradients and illustrations are subordinate to product explanation.

## Transferable Moves

- Show the system working, not just abstract benefits.
- Place business proof near the decision point.
- Use tables and diagrams for complex value instead of prose walls.
- Keep CTAs stable and specific.
- Use neutral surfaces with one confident accent.

## Do Not Copy

- Stripe's brand gradient language.
- Exact card compositions.
- Customer logos without real permission.
- Fake financial metrics.

## Checklist

- [ ] Product mechanics visible above the fold
- [ ] Proof is real or clearly marked fictional
- [ ] CTA says the outcome, not "Submit"
- [ ] Section rhythm alternates explanation and evidence
- [ ] Visual drama does not hide weak copy
