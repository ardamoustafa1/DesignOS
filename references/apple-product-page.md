# Reference Pack: Apple Product Page

Use for premium product storytelling, hardware-like software launches, and focused
consumer experiences.

## Taste Signature

- Radical simplification.
- One idea per viewport.
- Precise copy, high confidence, very few competing elements.
- Visuals carry the product story.

## Transferable Moves

- Remove anything that does not advance the product story.
- Let spacing create importance.
- Use motion only to clarify sequence or materiality.
- Make the product/object the first-viewport signal.
- Pair short claims with concrete demonstration.

## Do Not Copy

- Hero minimalism when the product needs explanation.
- Oversized type inside dense app surfaces.
- Black-glass aesthetic without product fit.

## Checklist

- [ ] One primary idea per section
- [ ] Product is visible, not implied
- [ ] Copy is short and concrete
- [ ] No marketing badges without proof
- [ ] Mobile preserves the story, not just the layout
