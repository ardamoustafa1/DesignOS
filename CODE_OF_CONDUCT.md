# Code of Conduct

DesignOS's review culture is adversarial toward *work* and respectful toward *people* —
the reviewer agent attacks findings, never authors. The community holds the same line.

## Expected
- Critique with specifics and reasons (the `CONTRIBUTING.md` evidence standard applies
  to conduct in reviews: "this violates spacing.md's 2× rule because…" — never "this is
  garbage")
- Assume competence; disagree with rules by challenging them through the rule-challenge
  issue template, with evidence
- Welcome newcomers: the bar is high for *rules*, not for *questions*

## Not tolerated
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissing contributions by identity rather than content
- Bad-faith participation (spam, self-promotion floods, LLM-generated filler PRs)

## Enforcement
Maintainers may edit, close, or remove contributions and contributors that violate this.
Report conduct issues privately via GitHub's report mechanisms or the security contact.

Adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant v2.1.
