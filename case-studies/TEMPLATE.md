## What Failed First

The strongest case studies include failures:

- contrast miss
- hierarchy issue
- fake proof removed
- missing state added
- mobile layout fixed

## Caveats

## Anti-patterns

- Presenting fictional brands as external adoption.
- Omitting mobile.
- Publishing only the polished final screenshot.
- Claiming conversion lift without real analytics.
