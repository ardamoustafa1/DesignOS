## Submission rules

Add one table row via PR:

```markdown
| [Project name](https://live-url) | sector/type | one honest sentence on what DesignOS changed |
```

1. **Live URL required** — screenshots rot, links get checked.
2. **The sentence is the value:** what did the system catch, enforce, or improve?
   ("The reviewer failed my hero contrast twice" beats "great tool!")
3. Your work stays yours — listing here grants us only the right to link and feature.
4. Entries that stop resolving get pruned at quarterly health checks
   (`loops/review-loop.md` rot detection — applied to ourselves).
5. No NSFW/scam/dark-pattern products — we check against our own
   `museum/README.md` Wing V, with irony fully intended.
